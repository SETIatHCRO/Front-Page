PAXController.oln - board outline and mounting holes
PAXController.slk - top silkscreen
PAXController.smt - top solder mask
PAXController.L1 - top layer
PAXController.L2 - second to top layer
PAXController.L3 - third to top layer
PAXController.L4 - third to bottom layer
PAXController.L5 - second to bottom layer
PAXController.L6 - bottom layer
PAXController.smb - bottom solder mask

units are imperial (inches) not metric!
